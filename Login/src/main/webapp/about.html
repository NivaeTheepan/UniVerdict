About Us

At UniVerdict, we recognize that university life can be both exhilarating and daunting. As students navigate their academic paths, they often encounter challenges that leave them feeling unprepared for the rigorous coursework. That's where we step in. At UniVerdict we bridge the gap between students and their course experiences. We've created website specifically for Computer Engineering students at Toronto Metropolitan University. Here's what we have to offer:

  • Course Ratings and Reviews: UniVerdict allows students from various years to provide ratings and share candid comments about their courses. Whether it's an introductory programming class or an advanced algorithms course, our platform lets you learn from your peers' experiences.
  • Professor Insights: While other platforms focus on professor ratings, we believe that understanding the course itself is equally crucial. Our website fills the void by emphasizing course-specific information. After all, a great professor can't compensate for a poorly designed curriculum.
  • Difficulty Scale: Wondering how challenging a course might be? UniVerdict provides a difficulty scale based on past students' feedback. Whether it's a breeze or a storm, you'll know what to expect.
  • Resource Hub: UniVerdict isn't just about reviews; it's about empowering your learning journey. We've curated a comprehensive resource hub with countless links to study materials. These resources, recommended by upper-year students, cover everything from textbooks to online tutorials. Say goodbye to endless Google searches!
